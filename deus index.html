FAQ

Questions we get asked.

How does the AI interview work? +
You have a conversation with our AI interviewer. It asks thoughtful questions about your expertise, adapting in real-time to go deeper. Each 10–15 minute session generates multiple LinkedIn® post drafts that sound like you.
Will my posts actually sound like me, not generic AI? +
Yes. SuperGrow builds a voice profile unique to you — your vocabulary, tone, opinions, and topics you're known for. Because content starts from a real conversation, the output captures how you actually think and speak.
How much time do I need to invest? +
A 10–15 minute conversation gives you a week's worth of LinkedIn® content. Most users spend under 30 minutes a week to stay consistently visible.
Is SuperGrow for individuals or teams? +
Both. Individual users get content creation, scheduling, and analytics. Teams get everything individuals do, plus multi-member management, approval workflows, challenges, and permissions.
